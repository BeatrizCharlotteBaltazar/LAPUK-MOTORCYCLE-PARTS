MACNA OCTAVIUS GLOVES

Description

The Octavius motorcycle glove consists of stretchy, breathable spandex and goat leather at the back of the hand. Goat leather and synthetic nubuck are used at the palm, and the cuff is made of soft, comfy neoprene. The fingertips are reinforced with an extra layer of leather. The absence of a waterproof membrane prevents you from sweating in warm riding conditions. Both the knuckles and palm are well protected by Macna's new Armax™ protectors. This construction leaves out the need for seams in critical areas, basically eliminating the weakest link, and thus greatly increasing your safety.

The Armax™ palm protector is fitted onto an extra layer of synthetic nubuck that reinforces the palm. Underneath the hard abrasion-resistant cover, soft and comfy temperfoam maintains comfort and provides additional impact absorption. The thumb is fitted with EVA, which is rubber-like in its finish and flexibility and provides impact absorption. At the fingers, you'll find TPR protection with similar characteristics.

Octavius features the Ergothumb™ construction, meaning the thumb and index finger are made of one and the same panel of fabric. This reduces seams and pressure points whilst also giving a wrinkle-free look. Thanks to the Touch Tip, you'll be able to operate touch screens without taking your gloves off.

Features

  • Mix of goat leather and stretchy, breathable spandex
  • Comfortable tricot liner
  • Suited for hot riding conditions
  • Touch Tip for touch-screen compatibility
  • Short, adjustable cuff
  • Ergothumb™

Specifications

  • Armax™ and Temperfoam knuckle protection
  • Armax™ palm protector
  • TPR fingers
  • EVA thumb padding
  • Reinforced palm
  • PPE - protective motorcycle gloves (EN 13594:2015, KP 1)

FIVE GLOVES MOTORCYCLE E-WP

Description

Because off-roading (Enduro, quad, ATV) is fun year-round, including when the weather’s bad, you have to be equipped and ready for anything. FIVE is proud to introduce the E-WP, an off-road glove that features a 5_DRYTECH™ breathable, weatherproof membrane and a water-repellent stretch outer shell. This glove is truly effective and comfortable when you have to face the wind and rain, which is sometimes a reality when you’re on long rides in the great outdoors.

Made from tough, durable materials that are moisture- and abrasion-resistant, the E-WP should always be in your belt bag or backpack when the weather’s likely to take a turn for the worse, so you can keep on rolling with your hands dry, at the first sign of rain. Its synthetic leather AX Suede® embossed palm, for enhanced grip on wet, or even mud-splattered, controls, offers excellent navigational control and significant comfort, especially when hours of riding seem endless in the pouring rain.

With its Touch Screen™ system and Clear Vision Pad™, you’ll always be able to use your GPS or wipe off the outside of your visor, to help you find your way. You may end up exhausted and soaked from head to toe…but your hands will stay nice and dry inside the E-WP.
