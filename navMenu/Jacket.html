MACNA VELOTURA MOTORCYCLE MESH JACKET

Description

The most accessible jacket for riders in hot conditions.

The Velotura is designed for warm riding conditions with a ton of 3D-mesh fabric to ensure enough airflow past your body, with impact areas like elbows and shoulders being the exception. The fit of the jacket can easily be adjusted by Velcro at the cuffs and hips.

To maintain that perfect fit, we’ve fitted our Belt Loop; a strong piece of webbing that enables easy connection between your jacket and belt, preventing the jacket from creeping up. Follow the Belt Loop all the way up, and you’ll find the Coat Hanger that’s designed to carry the weight of a motorcycle jacket.

Note: Product color may slightly vary due to photographic lighting sources or your monitor settings.
